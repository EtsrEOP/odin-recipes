The Mead of Poetry Recipe

Ingredients

  • Saliva of Aesir and Vanir Gods

  • Tablespoon of honey

  • Large Hollow Mountain

Steps

  1. Combine, with a 1 to 1 ratio, the Aesir and Vanir saliva in a medium saucepan

  2. After 3-5 minutes the being of wisdom called "Kvasir" will appear!

  3. Murder the being until even color and texture throughout, then transfer to a cauldron

  4. Add a Tablespoon of honey to the "mixture" for flavor

  5. Age the mead in a hollow mountain for 10 millennia

  6. Serve and enjoy!
