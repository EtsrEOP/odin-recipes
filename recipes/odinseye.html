Odin's Eye Stew Recipe

Ingredients

  • Thirst for Knowledge

  • Well of Wisdom

  • Eye (must be yours)

  • Sharp Knife

Steps

  1. Visit your local well of wisdom

  2. Take your knife and pluck out your favorite eye

  3. Let the eye soak in the well for 2 hours, seek medical attention

  4. Post surgery, Serve and Enjoy!
